Crataegus — Hawthorn (Meidoorn)

A battle-scarred hedgerow veteran bristling with thorns, it fights dirty with a laugh — the original barbed-wire barrier, ancient enough to have watched empires rise and fall from behind its spiky ramparts.

Combat flavor

Dense, slow-growing, and armored — Crataegus is a defensive specialist whose thorns make melee attackers think twice. Its long lifespan hints at a passive regeneration or fortification ability: old hedges that just won't die.
