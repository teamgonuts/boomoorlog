Morus — Mulberry (Morus)

A gnarled, silk-road veteran with centuries of history — slow to establish but immovable once rooted, and generous to every creature within reach of its fruit-laden arms.

Combat flavor

Mulberry's fast growth gives it rapid deployment speed, while its dense, durable wood backs up solid attack power; its "Silk Road" identity lends itself to a support ability that boosts economic or resource generation for the player's economy layer.

Real-world facts

Typical / max height10–20 m (M. alba); up to 18 m (M. rubra)
LifespanM. rubra <75 yr; M. alba 150+ yr; M. nigra 300+ yr (exceptional individuals documented over 500 yr)
Wood~690 kg/m³ air-dry (M. rubra ~688 kg/m³); ring-porous, golden-brown heartwood, naturally durable — comparable in density to red oak; moderately hard
Growth speedfast
Ecology / notable traitsFruits eaten by an enormous range of birds, mammals, and insects; M. alba introduced for silk-worm cultivation (leaves are sole food of Bombyx mori) — a living artefact of the global silk trade; highly adaptable to varied soils and pH; M. nigra considered rare/threatened in native range (central Asia); no serious allelopathy or toxicity; staining fruits a deep purple-red
