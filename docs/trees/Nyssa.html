Nyssa — Tupelo (Nyssa)

The oldest hardwood fighter in the eastern forest — wiry, interlocked-grained, and maddeningly hard to split; a shadow-tolerant survivor that outlasts foes by simply refusing to fall.

Combat flavor

The cross-grained, unsplittable wood makes Nyssa a classic skirmisher who absorbs glancing blows and keeps going; its exceptional longevity suggests a late-game endurance bonus. Signature ability candidate: Grain Lock — chance to deflect melee hits entirely due to the interlocked wood structure.
