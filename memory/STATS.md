# Genus Stats — Boomoorlog

One stat block **per genus** (~50 archetypes), NOT per individual tree.

## Combat model (locked 2026-06-27)
Trees are the **offensive** units. Two ZIP-code **armies of trees march toward each
other and clash** (auto-battler / marching armies), NOT a stationary tower defense.
Because they advance, **movement speed is a real stat**.

## The 5 core stats — two axes, all derived from Amsterdam data

Two independent axes from the data drive the stats so big trees are NOT auto-slow:

**SIZE axis** (height, trunk diameter) → power stats:
| Stat | Derived from |
|---|---|
| **Range** | avg height |
| **Attack** | avg trunk diameter (girth = punching power) |
| **Health** | mass proxy = height × diameter (tankiness) |

**VIGOR axis** (growth rate, independent of absolute size) → agility stats:
| Stat | Derived from |
|---|---|
| **Attack speed** | growth rate (height ÷ age) |
| **Movement speed** | growth rate (vigor) |

Payoff: a **fast-growing giant** (Poplar, Metasequoia) is big AND fast = elite killer.
A slow-growing giant (Oak) is devastating but ponderous. Small trees are quick but
feeble. Big trees are never auto-slow. Attack speed & Movement both come from vigor,
so they correlate (one "agility" axis) — accepted for simplicity.

## Data coverage notes
- Height: ~87% populated — strong basis for Range.
- Planting year (age): ~94% populated — strong basis for Attack & Movement.
- Trunk diameter: only ~29% populated — too sparse per-tree, but per-genus AVERAGE
  over the ~29% with data is usable as the archetype Health value.

## Rarity / world-rarity multiplier (locked 2026-06-27)
A power multiplier rewards **global / world rarity**, NOT Amsterdam rarity. A tree
that's rare in Amsterdam but common elsewhere in the world gets **no buff**. Only
genuinely globally rare / exotic / iconic species (e.g. Ginkgo, Metasequoia, Taxodium,
Pterocarya, Parrotia — living fossils, limited natural range) get a multiplier so a
unique exotic giant towers over commons. Default multiplier = 1.0.

## No splash (locked 2026-06-27)
Combat is **one-by-one**, no AoE/splash. Big trees dominate via high raw stats +
rarity multiplier, not area damage.

## Decisions locked
- **5 stats**: Attack, Range, Health, Attack speed, Movement. (Health = trees can die.)
- **Stats derived from Amsterdam data first and foremost** (size + vigor axes).
- **World-rarity multiplier** (not Amsterdam rarity); no "cost" stat.
- **No splash** — single-target.
