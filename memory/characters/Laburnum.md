# Laburnum — Laburnum (Laburnum)

**Archetype (draft):** Support
**Amsterdam trees:** 294

## Personality
A golden-chained beauty queen packing cytisine in every seed pod — it looks like a spring garden party and ends like a poison seminar, thriving on bare rock through nitrogen-fixing roots while everything around it wilts.

## Real-world facts
- **Typical / max height:** 7–9 m typical; up to 15 m maximum
- **Lifespan:** 30–50 years (relatively short-lived; grows fast and burns out)
- **Wood:** 801 kg/m³ air-dry; Janka ~1,340 lbf (green) — hard, close-grained, dark chocolate-brown heartwood; excellent turning and carving wood; historically used for furniture inlay and musical instruments
- **Growth speed:** Fast (up to 1 m/year; full height in 10–15 years)
- **Ecology / notable traits:** Highly toxic — all parts, especially seeds, contain cytisine (nicotinic agonist); fatal in large quantities; woodworking dust causes nausea and vomiting; nitrogen-fixing root nodules allow it to colonize poor soils; ecologically undemanding; spectacular yellow pendant flower racemes ("golden chains") attract bees; mildly invasive in some regions outside its native Alpine/Southern European range

## Combat flavor
Fast-growing but short-lived, Laburnum is a glass-cannon Support — it springs up quickly, dishes out poison-area denial with its seed pods, and relies on its nitrogen-fixing aura to accelerate allied growth, then burns out. A high-offense Support with a built-in timer.
