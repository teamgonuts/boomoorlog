# Liriodendron — Tulip tree (Liriodendron)

**Archetype (draft):** Bruiser
**Amsterdam trees:** 781

## Personality
The tallest hardwood in North America rockets skyward like a living siege tower — column-straight, absurdly fast, and covered in flowers that look like it's already celebrating victory.

## Real-world facts
- **Typical / max height:** 20–30 m in urban settings; 50–60 m in native Appalachian cove forests; exceptional specimens approach 58 m
- **Lifespan:** 250–300 years typical; maximum around 450–500 years
- **Wood:** ~455 kg/m³ (air-dry); Janka ~540 lbf; classified as a hardwood but one of the lightest and softest — workable, straight-grained; commercially sold as "yellow poplar"
- **Growth speed:** Fast (60–90 cm/year, up to 1.2 m in ideal conditions)
- **Ecology / notable traits:** Living fossil — genus dates to the Cretaceous; only two living species (L. tulipifera in North America, L. chinense in China), a classic Gondwana disjunct distribution; large, nectar-rich tulip-shaped flowers are a major pollinator resource; straight trunk with no lower branching gives extraordinary height-to-base ratio; intolerant of waterlogged soil; fast growth without the typical brittle-wood penalty; state tree of three US states

## Combat flavor
Light wood means fast attack speed despite its massive size — a Bruiser that trades armor for reach and speed, and its living-fossil status could unlock a "ancient lineage" legendary-tier ability.
