**Archetype (draft):** Bruiser
**Amsterdam trees:** 19757

## Personality
A hard-swinging workhorse forged into sports bats and tool handles, now swinging back — tough, shock-resistant, and furious about ash dieback tearing through the ranks.

## Real-world facts
- **Typical / max height:** 18–25 m typical; max ~43 m (Fraxinus excelsior)
- **Lifespan:** 150–250 years typical; rarely beyond 250
- **Wood:** 660 kg/m³ air-dry density; Janka ~1480 lbf — hard, dense, highly shock-resistant
- **Growth speed:** Fast
- **Ecology / notable traits:** Pioneer on fertile neutral soils; poor decay resistance (must fight in the open); beloved for tool handles, oars, and baseball bats due to shock absorption; catastrophically threatened by ash dieback (Hymenoscyphus fraxineus fungus, ~70% European mortality) — a warrior species fighting extinction

## Combat flavor
Dense, shock-absorbing wood means powerful, punishing blows; its fast growth feeds a large troop count, but the looming ash-dieback threat could translate to a "plague vulnerability" debuff. Signature ability candidate: **Resilient Swing** — absorbs incoming damage briefly before counter-striking harder.
