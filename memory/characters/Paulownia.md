# Paulownia — Foxglove Tree (Paulownia)

**Archetype (draft):** Skirmisher
**Amsterdam trees:** 208

## Personality
Blindingly fast, deceptively light, and gone before you can retaliate — Paulownia blitzes in with soft punches you almost laugh off, until you realise the lane is already lost.

## Real-world facts
- **Typical / max height:** 9–18 m typical; up to 25 m in ideal conditions
- **Lifespan:** 30–70 years; fast-lived pioneer; forestry rotations as short as 5–7 years
- **Wood:** Air-dry density ~270–440 kg/m³ (one of the lightest hardwoods on Earth; ~300 kg/m³ typical plantation wood); botanically a hardwood but softer and lighter than most softwoods; low Janka, approximately 660–900 N
- **Growth speed:** Fast — among the fastest trees in the world; 3–5 m/yr possible; 10–15 ft/yr in early years; can reach harvestable size in 5–7 years
- **Ecology / notable traits:** Native to China; naturalised and invasive in North America, southeastern Europe; produces showy purple foxglove-like flowers before leaves emerge; coppices vigorously (stumps re-sprout rapidly); nitrogen-tolerant; leaves are large (up to 60 cm on regrowth shoots) and phosphorus-rich, improving soil; culturally significant in China and Japan (planted at birth of a daughter, used for furniture and musical instruments).

## Combat flavor
Ultra-light wood means rapid-fire, low-mass strikes — a Skirmisher that darts in, lands a flurry, and retreats before the enemy can respond. Its coppicing ability could translate to a respawn or regeneration mechanic; its invasive spread suggests a lane-creep passive.
