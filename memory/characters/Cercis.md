# Cercis — Redbud (Cercis)

**Archetype (draft):** Support
**Amsterdam trees:** 228

## Personality
A nitrogen-fixing pacifist who feeds the soil around every ally, then erupts in shocking pink flowers straight out of bare wood — no leaves needed, just drama.

## Real-world facts
- **Typical / max height:** 6–12 m; typically a small spreading tree
- **Lifespan:** 50–70 years (some sources cite 20–25 years for stressed urban specimens; healthy trees reach 70)
- **Wood:** Hard, finely grained, beautifully veined — takes a high polish; used for decorative veneers. No reliable kg/m³ figure widely published; Janka for C. canadensis ~830 lbf (estimated from comparable legume hardwoods)
- **Growth speed:** fast when young (30–45 cm/yr), slows with age — overall medium
- **Ecology / notable traits:** Legume family (Fabaceae) — fixes atmospheric nitrogen, enriching surrounding soil; cauliflorous flowering (blooms erupt directly from trunk and branches, not just twigs); no thorns; tolerates poor, alkaline, and clay soils; native range southern Europe and SW Asia; culturally significant (Judas tree legend); attracts bees and early pollinators; notable but not globally rare

## Combat flavor
Nitrogen-fixing makes Cercis the premier soil-buffing support — a passive aura that boosts growth speed or HP regeneration of adjacent allies. Cauliflory (trunk-flowering) is visually bizarre enough to become a signature ability: blooms burst from its own bark mid-battle.
