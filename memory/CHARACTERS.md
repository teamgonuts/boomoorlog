## Archetypes (TD stat builds)

### Bruiser — Elite Carry
Big AND vigorous: strong, durable, and fast. The carnage units — a fast-growing giant that hits hard and keeps up.

### Juggernaut — Tank/Artillery
Big but slow: high Attack/Health/Range, low Attack speed & Movement. Lumbering heavy hitters / walls.

*Pterocarya* (Wingnut)★, *Aesculus* (Horse chestnut), *Tilia* (Linden), *Cupressocyparis* (Cupressocyparis), *Carpinus* (Hornbeam), *Salix* (Willow), *Ailanthus* (Tree of heaven), *Chamaecyparis* (False cypress)

### Skirmisher — Glass Cannon
Small but vigorous: fast move & attack, low Health/Range. Cheap swarmers that die fast.

### Support — Chaff/Filler
Low size and low vigor: weak filler that wins by sheer numbers in tree-dense ZIP codes.
