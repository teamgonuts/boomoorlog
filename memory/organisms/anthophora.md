Anthophora and Osmia are fast-flying solitary bees that visit Laburnum and other spring-flowering trees in Amsterdam; Osmia bicornis is particularly common on bee hotels in city gardens. They hover and bob between blooms in classic pollinator fashion. The flower-visitor / flower-bobber default fits cleanly.
