Black Medick is a low-growing annual or short-lived perennial legume of lawns, path edges, and grassy open areas, producing small yellow clover-like flower heads that are a useful nectar source for bees. It is a ground-hugging plant that thrives in disturbed, nutrient-poor soils — classic of park margins, verges, and trampled grassland throughout Amsterdam.
