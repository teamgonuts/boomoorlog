Elder is a fast-growing shrub or small tree found at the edges of parks, canal banks, waste ground, and beneath taller trees throughout Amsterdam; it is categorised as plant rather than tree in GBIF because it is a shrub-forming species. It produces flat-topped clusters of white flowers in June and purple-black berries in autumn, both highly attractive to insects and birds. The plant default of ground-park and none fits its placement in park margins and hedgerow edges. No override is needed.
