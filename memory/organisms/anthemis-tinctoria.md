Yellow Chamomile is an aromatic biennial or short-lived perennial in the daisy family with bright golden-yellow ray florets, historically used as a dye plant. In Amsterdam it grows on dry, disturbed ground, roadsides, and sunny park edges, thriving in well-drained sandy soils. Its current genus placement is Cota (formerly Anthemis).
