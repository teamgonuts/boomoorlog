Spear thistle is a biennial herb of rough grassland, park edges, disturbed ground, and roadsides throughout Amsterdam; it is one of the most common thistles in the city. Its large purple flower heads are highly attractive to bumblebees, butterflies, and goldfinches. The plant default of ground-park and none describes its placement well. No override is needed.
