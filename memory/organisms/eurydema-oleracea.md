The Cabbage Bug is a brightly patterned shieldbug (Pentatomidae) that feeds on brassicaceous plants, piercing stems, leaves, and seeds of wild and cultivated cabbages, hedge mustard, and related plants. In Amsterdam it is found on ruderal patches, road verges, allotments, and park margins wherever crucifers grow. Adults and nymphs move slowly between host plant stems and spend much of their time stationary.
