Small Teasel is a biennial herb that favours shaded, moist ground at woodland edges, along canal banks, and in park margins throughout the Netherlands. Unlike the common teasel it prefers shadier, damper situations and is locally scarce. As a non-tree vascular plant it takes the plant category default of ground-park and none, which accurately describes its placement at the base of hedges and park edges. No override is needed.
