Green Alkanet is a robust bristly perennial borage-relative with vivid blue forget-me-not-like flowers, widespread in Amsterdam as a garden escape established along canal banks, wall bases, shaded roadsides, and park margins. It thrives in the nitrogen-rich disturbed soil common at the foot of walls and fences in residential streets. The dominant habitat is ground-urban due to its characteristic presence at wall bases and pavement edges throughout the city, with ground-park secondary for its presence in park shaded margins. Movement stays none.
