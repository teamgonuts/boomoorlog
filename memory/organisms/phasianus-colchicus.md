The Common Pheasant is a large, heavy ground bird that forages by walking slowly through grassland, scrub, and woodland edges — it almost never perches in the canopy and is firmly ground-oriented. Although GBIF classifies it as a bird, the default tree-canopy / tree-flitter pairing is wholly wrong for a pheasant; it needs ground-park and park-roamer to match its actual behaviour. In Amsterdam it appears occasionally in larger parks and peri-urban green edges rather than inner-city gardens. Its leisurely foraging stride maps well onto the park-roamer animation.
