The dock bug is a large, brown coreid bug that feeds on the developing seeds of docks (Rumex) and sorrels, plants that grow abundantly in park margins, canal banks, and rough grassland throughout Amsterdam. It is a slow, cryptic feeder that relies on camouflage rather than rapid movement, making idle-only the appropriate animation. Ground-park is the dominant habitat because its host plants are almost always found in vegetated park-edge or rough ground settings. The flower-visitor insect default does not apply; it is a seed-sucker, not a pollinator.
