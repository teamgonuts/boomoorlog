A catch-all grouping of generalist aphids and soft scale insects that feed on the foliage and young shoots of a wide range of Amsterdam street and park trees: Ailanthus, Catalpa, Liquidambar, Populus, Pterocarya, and many more listed. They are sessile to slow-walking sap feeders that produce honeydew and sooty mould. Tree-canopy + idle-only fits the visible life stage; the flower-visitor default does not apply.
