Smooth Sow-thistle is an annual weed of disturbed, ruderal ground: pavement cracks, base-of-wall strips, waste sites, and garden edges throughout Amsterdam. It is predominantly an urban-ground coloniser rather than a park meadow plant, so ground-urban replaces the plant default of ground-park. The species germinates rapidly in any break in hard surfacing and is abundant along Amsterdam's street margins and canal-side walls. Movement remains none.
