This row aggregates greenfinch (Chloris chloris) and goldfinch (Carduelis carduelis) observations on Laburnum and Liquidambar street trees in Amsterdam. Both are small to medium finches that forage primarily in the canopy: greenfinches eat seeds from buds and Liquidambar fruits, goldfinches target seed heads. They are resident in Amsterdam year-round and move in small flocks between adjacent trees, matching the small-songbird default of tree-canopy / tree-flitter without modification.
