This entry groups chaffinch, goldfinch, greenfinch, siskin and various Sylvia warblers commonly observed in Chamaecyparis conifers in Amsterdam parks and cemeteries. All are classic small songbirds that forage and shelter within tree canopies and hop briskly between branches and neighbouring trees. The default bird tags of tree-canopy and tree-flitter fit this diverse grouping exactly. These species form the core of the bird default category in the taxonomy.
