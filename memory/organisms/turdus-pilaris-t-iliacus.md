Fieldfare and Redwing are winter visitors to Amsterdam that concentrate on Malus (crab apple) when available, alongside hawthorn and rowan. Both species gather in flocks to feed on fruit in the canopy and drop to park lawns for earthworms. Their tags mirror the turdus-pilaris entry — tree-canopy dominant for fruit feeding, ground-park secondary for earthworm foraging. Tags are identical for both species in this grouping as their habitat and movement overlap almost completely in the Amsterdam urban context.
