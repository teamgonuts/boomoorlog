The Green Immigrant Leaf Weevil is a small, bright metallic-green weevil that feeds on the fresh foliage of a broad range of deciduous trees including alder, willow, and hazel in Amsterdam parks and canal plantings. Adults are found directly on leaves in the canopy from spring through summer, making tree-canopy the clear dominant habitat. The default flower-visitor / flower-bobber tags do not fit a bark-and-leaf-feeding weevil; idle-only is appropriate because these beetles move slowly and spend most of their time chewing foliage rather than flying between flowers.
