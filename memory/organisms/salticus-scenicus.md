The Zebra Spider is a small but conspicuous jumping spider named for its bold black-and-white striped abdomen, most commonly seen hunting on sunlit exterior walls, window sills, fences, and paved surfaces in urban Amsterdam. It is a well-known city spider that actively stalks small flies by day, rotating its large forward-facing eyes to track prey before leaping. The default anywhere / idle-only partially fits, but wall-and-roof is more precise as the dominant habitat given its near-obligate use of built vertical surfaces. The BEHAVIOR_TAXONOMY notes it explicitly as a wall-and-roof example.
