These are tiny mites that graze sooty mould and fungal films on the leaves and bark of honeydew-laden trees like Liriodendron, Ulmus and Zelkova. They are essentially invisible to the naked eye and effectively stationary at the renderer's scale, so the arachnid default of anywhere / idle-only collapses neatly to tree-canopy in this tree-feeding context. The reason for override is purely to pin them to their host trees rather than scatter them across all habitats.
