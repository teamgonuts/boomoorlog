Blue Tits and Great Tits are among the most abundant and visible birds in Amsterdam's parks and street trees. They forage actively through the canopy of deciduous trees — especially oaks, maples, lindens, and elms — picking insects and invertebrates from leaf surfaces and crevices. Both species hop rapidly between branches and flit to neighbouring trees every few seconds, making them ideal candidates for the tree-flitter animation.
