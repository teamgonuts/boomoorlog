Hazel-feeding aphids including the large hazel aphid (Corylobium avellanae) and the common hazel aphid (Myzocallis coryli), found on Corylus throughout Amsterdam parks and ornamental plantings. Both are sap-feeders that cluster on leaves and shoots. Tree-canopy + idle-only is the correct grouping override.
