The garden snail is one of the most familiar molluscs in Amsterdam, found in parks, gardens, canal-side vegetation, and even pavement edges wherever there is damp soil and decaying plant matter. It is a generalist herbivore–detritivore that moves so slowly it is effectively stationary from an animation standpoint, matching the mollusc default of ground-park and idle-only. Activity is predominantly nocturnal or after rain. No override is needed; the category defaults describe this species accurately.
