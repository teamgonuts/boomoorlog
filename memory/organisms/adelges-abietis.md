Spruce gall adelgids form distinctive pineapple-shaped galls at the base of new shoots on Picea, particularly P. abies in Amsterdam park plantings and cemeteries. The sap-feeding insects live their winged life cycle largely inside galls or sessile on needles. Tree-canopy + idle-only is appropriate; flower-visitor / flower-bobber would be completely wrong for a needle-galling adelgid.
