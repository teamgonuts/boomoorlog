This general Arachnida entry covers spiders and harvestmen found on Ginkgo in Amsterdam. Ginkgo biloba is a popular Amsterdam street tree, and its broad canopy provides habitat for a variety of generalist web-building and wandering spiders as well as harvestmen (Opiliones) that shelter among leaf litter and bark crevices. Arachnida as a class is far too ecologically diverse for a specific habitat assignment — canopy orb-weavers, ground-hunting wolf spiders, and bark-dwelling jumping spiders are all present — so the anywhere default with idle-only movement is the correct catch-all. Per-species overrides would be appropriate if individual species were identified.
