The Uncertain is a common noctuid moth found across urban Amsterdam, active at night and resting motionless on bark, fence posts, and rough vegetation surfaces by day. Its cryptic grey-brown patterning provides excellent camouflage against bark and lichen-covered surfaces. Adults fly from June to August, coming readily to light traps, which accounts for many Amsterdam observations. The larvae feed on low-growing herbaceous plants including docks and plantains in parks and verges. Placing it on tree bark captures the typical daytime resting posture that observers actually photograph.
