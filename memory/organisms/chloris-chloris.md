Greenfinch and house sparrow are both common Amsterdam residents recorded on Styphnolobium japonicum (Japanese pagoda tree), a street tree that produces seed pods relished by seed-eating birds. Greenfinches are predominantly canopy feeders on seeds; house sparrows forage on the ground but also perch and feed in canopy. In the context of a Styphnolobium observation, both species are in the canopy, and the small-songbird default of tree-canopy / tree-flitter is a reasonable fit for the combined row. No override required.
