Mugwort is a tall, robust ruderal perennial that thrives on disturbed nutrient-rich ground throughout Amsterdam — road verges, railway embankments, brownfield sites, canal towpaths, and the margins of industrial areas. Its aromatic grey-green foliage and inconspicuous flowers make it a classic nitrogen-loving weed of built-up ground rather than a true park plant, justifying the ground-urban dominant tag. It does colonise unmown park edges, so ground-park serves as a secondary habitat.
