Tansy is a tall, strongly aromatic perennial herb native to temperate Europe that grows in rough grassland, roadsides, canal banks, and disturbed ground across Amsterdam. Its flat-topped clusters of bright yellow button flowers are a late-summer landmark in parks, railway margins, and brownfield sites.
