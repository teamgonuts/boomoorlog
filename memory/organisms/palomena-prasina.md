The Green Shieldbug is a phytophagous pentatomid that feeds on a wide range of herbaceous plants and shrubs in parks and garden edges, puncturing plant tissue to extract sap. It does not visit flowers for nectar and is not a pollinator, so the default flower-visitor / flower-bobber pairing does not fit. Adults spend most of their visible time sitting motionless on leaves — idle-only is the right movement. It uses both low park vegetation and shrubby tree-canopy margins, so a dual habitat of ground-park (dominant) plus tree-canopy reflects its full range without overstating mobility.
