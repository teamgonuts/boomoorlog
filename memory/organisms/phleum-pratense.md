Timothy is a tall, tufted perennial grass with a distinctive cylindrical, spike-like flower head — one of the most recognisable grasses in Amsterdam's parks, road verges, and unmown green strips. It is widely used in amenity grass seed mixes and establishes readily in park lawns that are not closely mown. The abundant pollen makes it a major hay-fever trigger in summer.
