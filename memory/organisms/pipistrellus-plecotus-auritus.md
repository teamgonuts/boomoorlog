Their shared tree-bark placement during daylight hours captures the roost site accurately, with sky-only as the active-flight habitat. The sky-looper animation is the dominant movement for the rendered dusk phase, and idle-only covers the stationary daytime roost. Both species override the mammal default of ground-park and park-roamer.
