The Flame is a small noctuid moth whose orange-brown bark-like pattern gives it superb camouflage when resting against dead wood, tree bark, or dry grass stems in parks and rough ground. Larvae feed on low herbaceous plants including grasses and docks in park margins. The default flower-visitor / flower-bobber tags are wrong for a bark-resting moth; ground-park is dominant for the larval habitat, and tree-bark is secondary for the adult's preferred resting surface. Idle-only is correct as adults are completely motionless by day.
