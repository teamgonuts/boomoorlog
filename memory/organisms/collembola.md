Bark-dwelling springtails (Collembola) are tiny hexapods recorded across a diverse range of Amsterdam tree genera including Cercidiphyllum, Metasequoia, Parrotia, Paulownia, Platanus, Populus, and Quercus. They live in bark crevices, feeding on algae, fungi, and decaying organic matter on the bark surface. Their movements are too small and fast to represent as positional sprite movement at the map scale, so idle-only is the practical animation choice. The insect category default of flower-visitor / flower-bobber is overridden because springtails are bark-microhabitat organisms, not flower visitors.
