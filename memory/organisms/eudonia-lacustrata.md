Eudonia lacustrata is a small crambid moth whose larvae feed on mosses and algae growing on tree bark, old walls, and shaded stone surfaces. Adults rest motionless on these same mossy surfaces during the day, blending into the texture. In Amsterdam they are recorded from shaded park trees, cemetery walls, and old brick buildings where moss has established.
