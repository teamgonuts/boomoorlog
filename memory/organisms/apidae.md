Apidae here covers honeybees (Apis mellifera) and early-emerging bumblebees (Bombus terrestris, B. pascuorum) that work the catkins of Corylus and the inflorescences of Morus in Amsterdam parks and gardens. They are the canonical flower-visiting pollinators the insect default was designed for. No override needed.
