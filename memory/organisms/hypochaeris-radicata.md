Cat's-ear is a common rosette-forming perennial of park lawns, meadow edges, and grassy path margins throughout Amsterdam. Its dandelion-like yellow flowers on branched stems are a reliable summer sight in Vondelpark and the Amsterdamse Bos. The default ground-park / none placement fits exactly: it grows in well-established grassy sward and does not favour bare pavement. The solid rosette and branched flowering stem would make a clear sprite for this species.
