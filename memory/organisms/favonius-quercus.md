The Purple hairstreak is an oak-specialist butterfly whose adults spend nearly their entire lives in the canopy of Quercus trees, rarely descending to ground level. In Amsterdam they are tied to street-side and park oaks, where they feed on honeydew secreted by aphids rather than seeking flowers. Their flight is a short fluttering movement among the upper branches, making flower-bobber the closest animation analogue despite the non-flower food source. The override from the insect default reflects their strict canopy residency and lack of interest in ground-level flowers.
