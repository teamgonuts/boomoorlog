A broader family-level grouping (Paridae) recorded on Liriodendron, Magnolia, Malus and Ostrya in Amsterdam. Blue Tit and Great Tit are the dominant members of this family in the Netherlands and their canopy habits are consistent across all four host genera. They glean insects from foliage and buds, flitting rapidly from branch to branch and tree to tree throughout the year. The default bird tags — tree-canopy habitat and tree-flitter movement — are accurate and no override is needed for this paridae aggregate slug.
