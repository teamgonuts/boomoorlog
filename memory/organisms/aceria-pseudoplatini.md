These eriophyid mites form the classic red bead-galls on sycamore and field maple leaves, common on Acer pseudoplatanus and A. campestre throughout Amsterdam. The galls are highly visible but the mites themselves never leave them. Tree-canopy + idle-only fits; override is to anchor placement to the host Acer rather than the arachnid default.
