This entry covers the leaf-litter and soil-surface community of springtails and oribatid mites beneath Fagus in Amsterdam. Unlike their bark-dwelling relatives, these organisms live in the decomposing beech leaf litter and humus layer on the ground. Ground-park is the correct habitat, placing the sprite in the park polygon rather than on the tree trunk. The insect category default of flower-visitor / flower-bobber is overridden because this is a litter/soil community; idle-only matches the below-threshold movement of mites and springtails at game sprite scale.
