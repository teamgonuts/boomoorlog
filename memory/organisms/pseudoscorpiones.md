Pseudoscorpions are tiny arachnids (1–5 mm) that live concealed in the deep bark furrows and under loose bark flakes of large urban trees such as Parrotia in Amsterdam, hunting mites and springtails within their micro-habitat. They are cryptic and virtually stationary from the perspective of any map-scale animation, sitting motionless in bark crevices for most of their lives. The arachnid default of anywhere / idle-only already captures their movement correctly, but the habitat needs an override from anywhere to tree-bark, which more precisely reflects where they are found. Idle-only movement remains unchanged.
