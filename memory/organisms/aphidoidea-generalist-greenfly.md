Generalist greenfly aphids occasionally recorded on Metasequoia glyptostroboides, a deciduous conifer planted as an ornamental in several Amsterdam parks. The aphids are sessile sap-feeders on young shoots. Tree-canopy + idle-only override consistent with other aphid groupings in this batch.
