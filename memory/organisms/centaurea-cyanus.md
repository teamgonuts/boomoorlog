The Cornflower is an annual herb of the daisy family with brilliant cobalt-blue ray florets, once a widespread arable weed now largely confined to wildflower plantings, park meadow areas, and road verges in Amsterdam. It grows on well-drained, nutrient-poor soils and has become a favourite for biodiversity planting schemes in Dutch cities.
