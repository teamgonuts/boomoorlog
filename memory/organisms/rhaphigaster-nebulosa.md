The Mottled Shieldbug is a large, grey-mottled pentatomid bug that feeds on the seeds and foliage of trees — particularly limes, elms, and oaks — making it a genuine tree-canopy species. It overwinters as an adult in bark crevices or leaf litter and resumes feeding in spring when new growth appears. In Amsterdam it is most likely to be seen on the foliage of large park and street trees.
