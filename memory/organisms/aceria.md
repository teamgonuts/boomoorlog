Eriophyid gall mites on Carpinus produce the small velvety erinea and leaf-margin rolls familiar on hornbeam in Amsterdam street plantings and park hedges. They live their entire life cycle inside leaf galls and never move at any visible scale. Tree-canopy + idle-only is the correct placement; the override exists so they sit on their Carpinus host rather than the arachnid generic anywhere default.
