Hedge Woundwort is a robust perennial herb of shaded, moist ground in woodland edges, hedgerows, and park margins throughout Amsterdam. It produces distinctive deep-purple hooded flowers on tall square stems in summer, attracting various bumblebees and solitary bees. The plant spreads by underground rhizomes and forms patches in the understorey of city parks and along canal-side vegetation. The default ground-park / none combination fits perfectly: it is a non-tree vascular plant rooted in vegetated soil that does not move.
