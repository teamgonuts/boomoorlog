The alder spittlebug nymph produces the conspicuous frothy "cuckoo spit" on alder shoots in early summer; adults are leafhopper-shaped and rest on foliage. Common on alders along Amsterdam waterways and in park plantings. Tree-canopy + idle-only fits the visible nymph and resting-adult stages better than the pollinator default.
