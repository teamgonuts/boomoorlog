Lady's Bedstraw is a low-growing perennial herb of dry, calcareous grassland, road verges, and sunny park margins; in Amsterdam it occurs mainly on sandy or gravelly ground in parks and along cycle-path edges. It produces clusters of small yellow flowers from June to September. The plant default of ground-park and none fits without modification. No override is needed.
