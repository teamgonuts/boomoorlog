Sphaerophoria is a genus of slender hoverflies whose adults are among the most reliable flower visitors in Amsterdam's parks and canal-side vegetation, hovering almost stationary over flower heads before darting to the next bloom. The larvae of most species are aphid predators in low herbaceous vegetation. Adults appear from spring through autumn and are particularly associated with yellow composites, umbellifers, and open sunny patches.
