Ribwort plantain is one of the most widespread plants in Amsterdam, found in virtually every park lawn, grass verge, and rough grassland patch in the city. Its narrow, ribbed leaves form basal rosettes and it tolerates mowing well. The plant default of ground-park and none describes its placement and animation accurately. No override is needed.
