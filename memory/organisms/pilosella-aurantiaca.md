Fox-and-cubs is a vivid orange-flowered hawkweed introduced from central Europe, now naturalised on lawns, verges, and park edges throughout Amsterdam. The clustered orange-red flower heads on hairy stems are immediately distinctive and unlike any native species. It spreads by stolons and seeds and can form dense patches in amenity grass. It is frequently recorded in Vondelpark and other larger green spaces. Default ground-park and none tags are correct.
