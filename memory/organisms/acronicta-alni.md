A grouping of alder-feeding moth caterpillars including the alder moth and the small eggar, whose communal silk tents are sometimes spotted on alder trees along Amsterdam's canal-side plantings. The visible life stage is the foliage-feeding larva, often resting in conspicuous postures. Tree-canopy + idle-only fits this larva-dominant grouping better than the pollinator-shaped insect default.
