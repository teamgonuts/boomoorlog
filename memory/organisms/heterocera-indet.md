Most moths recorded in Amsterdam parks rest on vegetation or bark during the day and are inactive unless disturbed. ground-park is the most conservative and broadly applicable placement for an unidentified moth in a park context. idle-only reflects the characteristic daytime resting posture shared by the vast majority of moth species.
