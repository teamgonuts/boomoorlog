The Nursery-web Spider is a hunting spider that stalks prey in tall grass, nettles, and low vegetation in parks, meadows, and woodland edges — it does not build a catching web and sits low in vegetation with a characteristic flat, body-to-substrate posture. Ground-park is the correct override from the arachnid default anywhere, since this spider is consistently found in ground-level and knee-height vegetation rather than on walls, bark, or flowers. It is largely sedentary between hunts, making idle-only the right movement class. In Amsterdam it is a common summer presence in longer grass in Vondelpark and Amsterdamse Bos, often seen carrying an egg sac.
